# Travel Plan: Kyoto, Japan

- **From:** Ha Noi, Viet Nam
- **Duration:** 5 days days
- **Travelers:** 4 people
- **Interests:** Places we can have fun and games, places with rich culture

---

### 🌟 **Final Travel Plan: 5 Days in Kyoto (4 People)**

---

## ✈️ **Trip Overview**  
This 5-day trip from **Hanoi, Vietnam to Kyoto, Japan** is designed for **cultural immersion and fun experiences**. You’ll explore **temples, museums, and traditional markets**, enjoy **interactive games and performances**, and savor **authentic Japanese cuisine**. The total budget of **$7,000** is **well within range**, with **flexibility for souvenirs and upgrades**.

---

## 📄 **Visa Information**  
- **Vietnamese citizens** can enter Japan **visa-free** for up to **90 days** for tourism.  
- **Valid passport** is required for entry.  
- No additional documents needed for short stays.

---

## ✈️ **Estimated Flight Cost**  
- **Round-trip flight (4 people)** from Hanoi to Kyoto: **$2,400–$3,200**  
- **Note**: Book early for better rates and consider **low-cost carriers** like VietJet or Japan Airlines.

---

## 🏨 **Accommodation (5 Days, 4 People)**  
- **Mid-range hotel or ryokan** in **Gion, Higashiyama, or Kagurazaka**: **$1,200–$2,000**  
- **Recommended**: Look for **family-friendly ryokans** for a cultural stay.

---

## 🗓️ **Day-by-Day Plan**

### **Day 1: Arrival & Cultural Immersion**  
- **Afternoon**: **Fushimi Inari Taisha** (red torii gates)  
- **Evening**: **Nishiki Market** (food & shopping) + **Kaiseki dinner**  

### **Day 2: Kyoto’s Cultural Heart**  
- **Morning**: **Kinkaku-ji & Ryoan-ji**  
- **Afternoon**: **Gion district** (geisha culture)  
- **Evening**: **Tea ceremony** at a local tea house  

### **Day 3: Fun & Games**  
- **Morning**: **Kyoto Animation Museum**  
- **Afternoon**: **Koi-koi game**, **sumo show** or **karaoke**  
- **Evening**: Free time for shopping or nightlife  

### **Day 4: Nature & Temples**  
- **Morning**: **Arashiyama Bamboo Grove & Katsura Imperial Villa**  
- **Afternoon**: **Hana no Yashiki** or **Tamba-dera**  
- **Evening**: **Kiyomizu-dera** and city view  

### **Day 5: Departure & Final Shopping**  
- **Morning**: **Kyoto Station Building** (shopping)  
- **Afternoon**: Final meal in **Gion or Higashiyama**  
- **Evening**: Departure  

---

## 🍣 **Food Suggestions**  
- **Must-try**: Kaiseki, yudofu, matcha desserts, katsudon, sushi  
- **Recommended Areas**: Nishiki Market, Gion, Higashiyama

## 📌 **Final Notes & Tips**  
- **Best Time to Visit**: April–October for weather and festivals.  
- **Transport**: Use **Kyoto City Bus** or **JR Pass** for convenience.  
- **Language**: Learn basic Japanese phrases for smoother interactions.  
- **Safety**: Kyoto is safe for travelers.  
- **Culture**: Respect temples by removing shoes and avoiding sacred areas.
